background.

I was born and raised in Saint Louis, Missouri, but then lived in Austin, Texas for 9 years where I grew up, enjoyed seeing tons of bands and drank beer all while being tossed around by the .com bubble. Once that was over I moved back to Saint Louis where I live with my wife, two children and two dogs. I am currently employed at a large company, the third such assignment since being back, as a Linux adminstrator. The environment is a big change from favorite gig in Austin where sandle clad developers and I worked on software at a 54 person company. I got to manage the code and administrate Unix and Linux servers all while keeping the mood light.
